For the man who has already mastered the basics, our barbers share the tools and products they use for the very best results.

The art and skill of shaving is celebrated throughout our shaving collection. From our Windsor Badger Shaving Brush, crafted in England, for a smooth and close shave even on the most sensitive skin, to our stainless steel Elliot Wood Handle Shavette, for a cut throat traditional shaving experience at home, master the art of the close shave and define your style. Retaining the heritage of traditional wet shaving, we’ve carefully chosen ingredients in our shaving supplies that soothe, nourish and replenish the skin. Our luxury Pre Shave Oil made with natural ingredients, softens whiskers and prepares your skin for the razor’s edge. Browse through our range of traditional wet shave razors and accessories online.

Treat yourself or a loved one to one of our traditional wet shave starter kits featuring softening Pre Shave oil, soothing Post Shave Balm and Avalon Shave Cream. For any shaving nicks, that can often happen when you’re in a rush or you haven’t stretched your skin before you shave, use our handy Alum Bar. The antibacterial remedy will effectively close your pores and soothe your skin. Visit us in London for a traditional cut throat wet shave experience, combining our effective cleansing and exfoliating products, our signature Avalon Shave Cream and our moisturising Post Shave balm for the ultimate close shave, that won’t irritate or damage your skin.

We now offer free worldwide delivery on all Murdock orders over £70, so feel free to shop online and find the perfect shaving gift or experience for you or a loved one.
